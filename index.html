Tap Blast! - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Introduction

Tap Blast! ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile puzzle game developed by Ahmet Dogan Bayindir. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Tap Blast! itself does not directly collect any personal information. However, the app uses third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and usage data to serve relevant ads. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Game Center: If you choose to use Game Center for leaderboards and achievements, Apple may collect your Game Center profile information. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Advertising

Our app displays banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded video ads through Google AdMob. You may opt out of personalized advertising by:

  • Declining the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt when first opening the app
  • Going to Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking on your iOS device

You can also purchase "Remove Ads" through an in-app purchase to disable all advertisements.

In-App Purchases

Tap Blast! offers an optional in-app purchase to remove advertisements. All purchases are processed securely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Data Storage

Game progress, high scores, and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this data to any external servers.

Children's Privacy

Tap Blast! is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The ads shown are not targeted based on children's data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
